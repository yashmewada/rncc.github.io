What is Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyaan?

Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyaan is a vision of making India self- reliant. In simple words, the point is to manufacture and utilize the products made in India and further promote it.

  • Current Situation

    • Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyaan acting as a chance for Indian startups to take charge of the innovations, for which we otherwise wpuld depend on global suppliers.

  • Here's an Example:

    • The success story of adivasi women in Chattisgarh is one of the many examples of self reliant India. Because of the spread COVID-19 globally, it had caused shortage of hand sanitizers amongst other products. So this women from Chattisgarh developed a brand named 'Madhukam' (Herbal Hand sanitizer) with guidance from a scientist, researcher and a consultant, Samarth Jain.

Why there's a need to take up this act?

Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyaan has given us schemes to boost manufacturing, upgrading and taking initiatives for improving the quotient of ease ny doing business and so on.

In other words it's a must to take up this act because for a long long time, India has been depended upon the outside countries for most products. That has some way also caused a loss for our country.

Keeping in mind that, the initiative of Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyaan is a must, because the schemes provided are just perfect to help boost our manufacturing power, the innovations techniques and our creativity.

How can we become Atmanirbhar?

The whole point of Atmanirbhar Bharat is to develop our sense of creativity, the innovation and enhance skills etc, not just to talk about, but to actually put in practice.

  • We can achieve this by focusing on the five main pillars, especially Economy, in this we need to focus on manufacturing in our country, on how to create raw materials here itself. Also when we talk about education, we need to focus on how to develop the interest and skills of the student or provide better technological knowledge.
  • In the end, once we focus on one quality or skill and keep working on it, further be specialist, we will be able to achieve the best results, success, oppurtunities and thus enhancing the potential of the Country.

Vocal For Local

With this current situation and initiative of this abhiyaan it is now more then ever we need to support local business, if not us then who will? The thought behind this is all the leading brands in the world today were once local brands, they achieved success because of people. People used and recommended the products, in this way they got a brand name. Similarly, we should also start using and talking about the products which fall under local market.

For Example: Recently many people have started small baking business or selling of hand made masks etc, we should try and promote that, for it gives them a chance to earn and respect their efforts.

Aids provided by the Government for Atmanirbhar Bharat

Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyaan's main focus is on the five pillars that includes Economy, Infrastructure, System, Vibrant demography and Demand.

Benefits are:

  • Provide relief package for the poor to help them foght the global crises.
  • The Reserve Bank of India will Implement some measures to help in increasing the cash liquidity in the market.
  • The government has provided certain relaxation to both employer and employee.
  • Provide the contracters with extending timelines and better cash liquidity.
  • Packages also for farmers who toil in different weather conditions and for the middle class who pay thier taxes on time.
